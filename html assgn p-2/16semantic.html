SJDC Article

St. Joseph's Degree College, located in Kurnool, India, is governed by St. Joseph's Educational Society and registered under the Societies Registration Act XXI of 1860 in 1967. St. Joseph's Degree College is a self-financed college, without grant-in-aid.

The Vision of our founders is reflected in the vision of our college that is to impart quality education upholding values of Indian culture. To prepare the young men and women take up the challenges of this age with dedication and commitment.

Mission of SJDC

To prepare the youth of 21st century imparting need based education that enhances not only livelihood but also value based education that gives roots and wings as well, by promoting International brotherhood, environmental consciousness, gender justice and sense of harmony.
